## Topics covered

## What I understood
- The `GROUP BY` statement allows to create subsets of a relation.
- It is possible to also apply apply aggregate operators to subgroups of tuples to produce summary information about each group.
- Rollups are also useful to generate substotals and totals for the groups of tuples.
- It is important to remember to establish the subset attribute(s) when using aggreate operators, or you'll get an `Error 1140`.
- The `HAVING` clause allows to retrieve values of the aggregation statement only from groups that satisfy certain conditions.
- The `HAVING` clause is the `WHERE` clause for aggregate operators. (If actually using the `WHERE` clause, you would get an `Error 1111`)
